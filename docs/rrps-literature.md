# RRPS Literature Memo

## Literature Sweep (verified 2026-06-16)

Scope: verifies the prior-art and novelty claims in `docs/rrps-design.md` (§1, §9) and `docs/rrps-dreaming.md` (Part VI) for the title-B contribution — *a value-of-computation (VOC) controller resource-rationally allocating synthesis-and-scoring compute over **model space**, scored by an exact marginal-evidence oracle*. Two camps were searched adversarially: (1) the VOC / resource-rational metareasoning lineage and its 2024–2026 instantiations, and (2) LLM-driven probabilistic-program / Bayesian-model-structure discovery. Only citations the research agents actually surfaced appear below.

### New since the design docs

These works post-date or were not cited in `rrps-design.md` §9. Labeled by effect on the title-B novelty claim.

**Threatens (must cite and distinguish — the proposer/scoring lineage):**
- **BoxLM — Automated Statistical Model Discovery with Language Models.** Li, M. J., Vértes, E., et al. (2024), arXiv:2402.17879 (ICML 2024). The single closest **object-level** match and the direct predecessor of the RRPS proposer: an LLM iteratively proposes statistical models as probabilistic *programs* inside Box's Loop and critiques them. Differs on the controller: it allocates by a **fixed iteration count** + a temperature explore/exploit knob, has no metareasoning over #proposals × scoring-depth × stop, no exact marginal-evidence oracle (uses LLM critique), and no net-utility frontier vs enumeration/meta-greedy. Concede explicitly as the proposer lineage to avoid an "undisclosed nearest neighbour" rejection.
- **ModelSMC — A Probabilistic Framework for LLM-Based Model Discovery.** Anonymous (2026), arXiv:2602.18266. The most dangerous **contemporaneous** work: LLM-proposed simulator-model particles weighted by an **estimate of the marginal likelihood** — two of the paper's three ingredients. Decisive gap: a **fixed budget** (predetermined K iterations, N particles); its only adaptive lever is a clone-probability α for explore/exploit. No VOC, no allocation metareasoning, no stopping policy, no frontier-dominance. It is precisely the fixed-budget regime the controller claims to dominate. Load-bearing distinction: ModelSMC's evidence is **estimated (NLE)**; RRPS's must be **exact** — if any part of the RRPS pipeline estimates the evidence, the distinction from ModelSMC collapses.
- **RefineStat — Efficient Exploration for Probabilistic Program Synthesis.** Anonymous (2025), arXiv:2509.01082. LLM-driven PP synthesis with an iterative refinement loop that resamples prior/likelihood components when diagnostic-reliability checks fail. "Efficiency" is **diagnostic-aware heuristic** refinement, not VOC; scoring is by posterior-diagnostic metrics, **not** marginal evidence; no metareasoner, no frontier. Differs on **both** the controller and the scoring criterion.
- **Automated Machine Learning, Bounded Rationality, and Rational Metareasoning.** Hüllermeier, Mohr, Tornede & Wever (2021), arXiv:2109.04744 (ADS@ECML-PKDD 2021). The closest **framing** precedent and the most likely reviewer counter-citation: casts AutoML pipeline/algorithm search as meta-level deliberation under rational metareasoning. Does **not** defeat the claim — object = discriminative ML-pipeline composition (not generative-model structure), selection by predictive performance (not exact evidence), and it is a **position/framing paper**, not a built-and-measured VOC controller. Distinguish as "metareasoning over ML pipelines, not generative-model structure."

**Strengthens (timeliness + lineage; orthogonal object levels):**
- **Learning How Hard to Think: Input-Adaptive Allocation of LM Computation.** Damani, M., Shenfeld, I., Peng, A., Bobu, A., Andreas, J. (2025), ICLR 2025, arXiv:2410.04707. Establishes that adaptive VOC-style allocation works in the LLM era: trains a model of the **marginal benefit** of extra compute and routes it to the queries that benefit most (25–50% compute savings at fixed reward). Strong precedent for "adaptive VOC allocation works," but the object level is **per-query LM decoding** — exactly the distinction the paper rests on (model/program space vs decoding).
- **Static and Dynamic Values of Computation in MCTS.** Sezener & Dayan (DeepMind), UAI 2020 (PMLR v124), arXiv:2002.04335. Static-vs-dynamic VOC estimators for MCTS; the cleanest bridge citation showing the myopic-vs-non-myopic VOC distinction (and its under-exploration failure mode) stayed an active, formally studied problem after 2018 — strengthens the lineage underpinning the Hay/Russell-Tolpin-Shimony claim. Object level = search-tree expansion.

**Neutral context (cite for "VOC is alive in 2024–26"; neither confirms nor threatens):**
- **Rational Metareasoning for Large Language Models.** De Sabbata, Sumers, Griffiths et al. (2024), arXiv:2410.05563. Operationalizes VOC as a reward penalizing unnecessary reasoning (Expert Iteration); cuts 20–37% of reasoning tokens at equal accuracy. Object level = chain-of-thought length, not program/model space.
- **Effort Allocation for Deadline-Aware Task and Motion Planning: A Metareasoning Approach.** (2024), arXiv:2410.05828. VOC-style effort allocation for robotics TAMP under deadlines; parallels the project's anytime/streaming-SMC axis, orthogonal object level.

### Novelty verdict (from red-team) + recommended framing

**Verdict: narrow-but-defensible.** The exact intersection RRPS claims is genuinely unoccupied — no work crosses the two camps — but it is narrow, and the claim is killable if framed loosely. Each individual conjunct (LLM proposer; marginal-evidence scoring; VOC allocation) is well precedented, and two papers (ModelSMC, BoxLM) sit one component away. A reviewer can argue "obvious composition of established VOC + established LLM model-discovery," and a literal "never" would be falsified on a loose reading.

**Three differentiators that survive a hostile read:**
1. **Object level = model space.** No VOC paper — Russell-Wefald 1991, Hay/Russell/Tolpin/Shimony 2012, Callaway et al. 2018, nor the 2024–26 LLM instantiations (decoding, reasoning, pipelines, strategies) — has placed the controller over a space of probabilistic programs / generative-model structures.
2. **Exact marginal-evidence oracle** (analytic, 7 conjugate families, cross-checked ~5e-7 vs `nn-marginal-closed` + high-N IS), vs ModelSMC's estimated NLE, RefineStat's diagnostics, BoxLM's critique, and AutoML's predictive accuracy. **Load-bearing — must be airtight**: if any part of the pipeline estimates the evidence, do not call it "exact" and the distinction from ModelSMC collapses.
3. **Measured net-utility frontier-dominance** over **both** fixed-budget enumeration **and** meta-greedy, with seeds + bootstrap CIs. No competitor reports a dominance frontier; this is the one result no reviewer can dismiss as "obvious composition."

**Recommended framing.** Soften any "close the loop" / implicit "first ever" to *"to our knowledge, the first to place an explicit value-of-computation controller over **model space**, scored by exact marginal evidence."* Pre-empt both camps in related work: distinguish Camp 1 (BoxLM, ModelSMC, RefineStat) on the **controller** — "these systems fix the search budget; none reason about the value of further proposing or scoring; they are exactly the fixed-budget regime our controller dominates" — and Camp 2 (Russell & Wefald 1991; Hay/Russell/Tolpin/Shimony 2012; Callaway et al. 2018; Learning How Hard to Think 2025; Rational Metareasoning for LLMs 2024; AutoML-as-metareasoning 2021) on the **object level** — "VOC has controlled tokens, strategies, and pipelines; never generative-model structure." Make the two load-bearing differentiators bulletproof: contrast the **exact** oracle against ModelSMC's NLE / RefineStat's diagnostics in one sentence, and make **frontier-dominance with CIs** the headline empirical claim. Apply the attribution fixes (Hay/Russell/**Tolpin/Shimony** 2012, *Theory and Applications*; Callaway et al. author order). Position the contribution as the **synthesis + the measured dominance**, never any single ingredient.

### Bottom line for the paper

Title-B's contribution remains **defensible, but narrowly, and the defense lives entirely in two load-bearing facts** rather than in any individual ingredient. The literature sweep confirms every cited foundation (Russell-Wefald 1991, Callaway et al. 2018, Lieder & Griffiths 2020) and both Saad-2019 properties (fixed uniform MCMC budget; collapsed marginal-likelihood scoring) the gap rests on, and an adversarial two-camp search found **no work that crosses VOC metareasoning into probabilistic-program / Bayesian-model-structure space** — so the precise novel slice is genuinely unoccupied. But contemporaneous near-neighbours have closed in: ModelSMC (Feb 2026) lands two of the three ingredients (LLM proposer + marginal-evidence weighting) under a fixed budget, and BoxLM is a direct proposer predecessor — both must be conceded and distinguished, or the paper risks an "undisclosed nearest neighbour / obvious composition" rejection. The claim therefore stands **only** if (i) the evidence oracle is genuinely **exact** (not estimated anywhere in the pipeline — this is what separates it from ModelSMC) and (ii) the measured **net-utility frontier-dominance over both fixed-budget enumeration and meta-greedy carries a CI-lo > 0 win**. With those two facts airtight and the absolute "never" softened to "to our knowledge," title-B is publishable as the *synthesis-plus-measured-dominance*; without the CI-lo > 0 win it should drop honestly to title-A (the Saad-2019 re-instantiation on the GFI/MLX substrate), exactly as the design doc's §4 honest gate already prescribes.
